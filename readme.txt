EquipBestItem Reloaded 
A GUI based auto equip best item mod. 
Made for Mount & Blade II Bannerlord September 18 2023 v1.1.6 release

Installation:
1. Copy files to Mount & Blade II Bannerlord/Modules
2. Make sure EquipBestItem.dll is unblocked in the EquipBestItem/bin/Win64_Shipping_Client/
3. Run Mount & Blade II Bannerlord Launcher
4. Press mods and select EquipBestItem
5. Press play and confirm

Controls:
Left Ctrl - Hold to increment/decrement filter values by 10
Left Shift - Hold to increment/decrement filter values by 5
Left Alt - Hold to hide the filter buttons in the inventory slots
